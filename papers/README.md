# Papers for Do an 1

Downloaded papers for the project:

1. `01_Vincent2008_DenoisingAutoencoders.pdf`
   - Title: Extracting and Composing Robust Features with Denoising Autoencoders
   - Why read: Foundational paper for denoising autoencoders.
   - Source: https://icml.cc/2008/papers/592.pdf

2. `02_Vincent2010_StackedDenoisingAutoencoders_JMLR.pdf`
   - Title: Stacked Denoising Autoencoders: Learning Useful Representations in a Deep Network with a Local Denoising Criterion
   - Why read: Classic extension of DAE, useful for theory/background section.
   - Source: https://www.jmlr.org/papers/volume11/vincent10a/vincent10a.pdf

3. `03_Mascharka2017_DeepConvolutionalAutoencoder_MNIST.pdf`
   - Title: A Deep Convolutional Auto-Encoder with Pooling-Unpooling Layers in Caffe
   - Why read: Convolutional autoencoder on image data, close to your MNIST setting.
   - Source: https://arxiv.org/pdf/1701.04949.pdf

4. `04_Gondara2016_JointVisualDenoisingAndClassification.pdf`
   - Title: Joint Visual Denoising and Classification Using Deep Learning
   - Why read: Very close to your step 2 idea of proving denoising quality via classification accuracy.
   - Source: https://arxiv.org/pdf/1612.01075.pdf

5. `05_Soltani2023_EnergyEfficientReconfigurableAutoencoder_FPGA.pdf`
   - Title: An Energy-Efficient Reconfigurable Autoencoder Implementation on FPGA
   - Why read: Directly relevant to mapping autoencoder-style models onto FPGA.
   - Source: https://arxiv.org/pdf/2301.07050.pdf

6. `06_Zhang2021_FPGA_BilateralGrid_RealTimeDenoising.pdf`
   - Title: An FPGA-Based Fully Pipelined Bilateral Grid for Real-Time Image Denoising
   - Why read: Strong hardware-denoising reference for architecture and throughput discussion.
   - Source: https://arxiv.org/pdf/2110.07186.pdf

7. `07_Umuroglu2016_FINN_BNN_FPGA_Inference.pdf`
   - Title: FINN: A Framework for Fast, Scalable Binarized Neural Network Inference
   - Why read: Important FPGA deployment reference for neural inference and dataflow ideas.
   - Source: https://arxiv.org/pdf/1612.07119.pdf

8. `08_Goyal2021_FixedPointQuantization_CNN_Embedded.pdf`
   - Title: Fixed-point Quantization of Convolutional Neural Networks for Quantized Inference on Embedded Platforms
   - Why read: Useful for your fixed-point / quantization stage.
   - Source: https://arxiv.org/pdf/2102.02147.pdf

9. `10_Marquez2024_TrainableFixedPointQuantization_FPGA.pdf`
   - Title: Trainable Fixed-Point Quantization for Deep Learning Acceleration on FPGAs
   - Why read: More recent FPGA-focused quantization reference.
   - Source: https://arxiv.org/pdf/2401.17544.pdf
